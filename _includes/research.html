Research

The team is devoted to foundational innovations in the fields of Knowledge Graphs, Large Language Models and a variety of their applications.

Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge Graphs (KGs) model world knowledge in graph-like forms to facilitate search, query answering, recommendation, or analytics. Areas of our research include: automatic KG construction, neural reasoning with KG, KG augmented deep models, etc.

Large Language Models

Large Language Model (LLMs) are deep models pre-trained on large-scale corpus with general purposes. Areas of our research include: knowledge-aware prompts, model editing, language models reasoning, low-resource model training, etc.

AI in Science

Language and graph technologies are powerful in modeling complex scientific knowledge in domains such as life science, material science, or social science. We explore the integration of KGs and LLMs to advance AI scientific discovery.
